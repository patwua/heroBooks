## Quick checklist
☐ Correct invoice headings and identifiers.  
☐ Rate and amount shown; inclusive math uses r/(100+r).  
☐ Zero‑rated/exempt clearly indicated with evidence.  
☐ Totals foot and reconcile to ledgers.
