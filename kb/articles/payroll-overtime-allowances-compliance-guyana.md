## Journals (generic)
Wages: Dr Wages / Cr Statutory payables / Cr Bank.  
Allowances (taxable): Dr Wages / Cr Allowances payable.

## Pitfalls
Back-dated changes; misclassifying taxable benefits; not applying caps.
